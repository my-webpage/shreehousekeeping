Why Choose Us? 

  • Our Services - Cleanliness without pain
  • Workers will come at a fixed time.
  • Workers will work as per the check-list provided as per the housekeeping company.
  • The wing will be swept all days in a week and once in a week it will be mopped dry with water.
  • The ground will be always kept clean.
  • Once in a month from terrace to staircase, wall and roof will be swept with long-broom (this will be done as per your convenience).
  • If there is availability of water then the concrete-ground will also be cleaned once in a month.
  • The dust-bins will be cleared from each house at a particular time before the Garbage-truck comes.
  • The dustbins are cleared before the Garbage-truck come because these garbage starts to stink after some time and the people surrounding it mainly the ground floor and first floor people and the shops near that face problems. Dogs and scavengers also try to remove garbage for their consumption and use and it’s thrown on the streets.

  • The concern of the secretary and the committee will be minimized.
  • Secretary or committee can enquire the commercial and professional aspect of House-keeping anytime they feel like.
  • Bill for the completed assignment shall be submitted to the concerned person.
  • Each worker will carry our company’s identity card.
